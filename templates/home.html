What is E-Summit?

E-Cell SASTRA presents E-Summit '19,
a two-day window to break away from the conventions of the world.
E-Summit is your opportunity to live the entrepreneurial dream.
With events like Launchpad which help you start your long dreamt startup
to an expo where you can showcase the best of your abilities,
E-Summit is filled with opportunities, ideas, creativity and fun.





Launchpad


E-Cell Sastra’s flagship event,
Launchpad is the biggest platform to convert your burning idea into reality.
It is the perfect stage for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their big dream and get funded.
The ultimate destination for creative minds to collaborate together to change the way things work or to create a dent in the universe or to explore the boundaries of practical possibilities.
With investors carrying heavier wallets this time around, the stakes are higher than ever.
